Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account we collect your name and email address. As you use the Service, we also store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and breakdowns, and sending transactional emails such as password reset requests. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information and all associated expense records will be permanently removed within 30 days. We recommend exporting your data before requesting deletion.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure and analytics (for example, a cloud hosting provider). These services are contractually bound to handle your data only as necessary to support the Service and may not use it for their own purposes. We do not integrate with advertising networks or social media tracking pixels.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request deletion of your data, please reach out to us at any time.

Privacy enquiries: privacy@spendly.app
